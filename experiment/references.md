### Textbooks

**1. Digital Design**<br/>
BY: Morris Mano and Michael Ciletti - Chapter 5 (Sequential Logic)<br/>
_Comprehensive coverage of latches, flip-flops, timing analysis, and sequential circuit design._

**2. Digital Logic and Computer Design**<br/>
BY: Morris Mano - Chapter 5<br/>
_Fundamental concepts of sequential logic including SR, D, JK, and T flip-flops with timing diagrams._

**3. Digital Electronics: Principles, Devices and Applications**<br/>
BY: Anil K. Maini - Chapter 7<br/>
_Practical approach to sequential circuit design with detailed analysis of memory elements._

**4. Digital Systems: Principles and Applications**<br/>
BY: Ronald J. Tocci, Neal S. Widmer, and Gregory L. Moss - Chapter 5<br/>
_Excellent coverage of latches, flip-flops, and their applications in counters and registers._

**5. Computer Organization and Design**<br/>
BY: David A. Patterson and John L. Hennessy - Chapter 4<br/>
_Hardware perspective on memory elements and their role in processor design._
